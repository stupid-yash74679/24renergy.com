about 24R Energy

Empowering a Sustainable Future with Solar Energy


At 24R Energy, we specialize in transforming industrial rooftops and open spaces into efficient power plants, enabling businesses to significantly reduce energy expenses while promoting environmental sustainability. Established in 2018, we have become a leading provider of industrial solar solutions, committed to revolutionizing the renewable energy sector.


Our comprehensive services include Engineering, Procurement, and Construction (EPC) for industrial solar installations. By adopting our tailored solutions, clients can save up to 90% on their monthly energy costs. As an approved installer under the SURYA Gujarat initiative, we are proud to contribute to the state's efforts in streamlining solar energy adoption.
